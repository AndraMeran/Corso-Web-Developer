Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberies, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberies, and creamy brie. I will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons istant yast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus mor for the pan

For the topping

  • 4 cups (500 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons istant yast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus mor for the pan

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.
